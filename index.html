most popular sports bikes in bangladesh

Yamaha R15 V3

Yamaha R15 V3 is the most recent entry into the next generation competition, carrying the most powerful 155.1cc engine that is good for 19.04 BHP at 10000 rpm and 14.7 Nm at 8500 rpm. The motorcycle weighs the highest at 137 kg and carries a race spec seat height of 815 mm. All the bikes here are equipped with LED headlights and taillights. It carries the widest rubbers and comes out as the only option with USD front forks.

Honda CBR150R

Honda CBR150R comes with a relaxed engine that produces 16.9 BHP at 9000 rpm and 13.7 Nm at 7000 rpm. It comes mated to a 6-speed gearbox and claims wet weight of 135 kg. The design inspiration for all the three bikes lies somewhere in the old generation liter class products of the respective brands. Honda offers a relaxed ride height of 787 mm. Every bike here carries dual disc brakes that are not equipped with ABS.

Taro Gp1 V3

Taro Gp1 V3 is a newly designed and improved motorcycle from the Italian brand Taro. The fairing and headlight unit design is inspired from the Aprilia RSV4. This bike can produce 19 Bhp maximum power and 16.5 Nm of torque. For the EFI engine, this bike can deliver up to 40 km/l average mileage and 135 km/h top speed.

Suzuki GSX-R150

Suzuki GSX-R150 is the lightest in its class, carrying a wet weight of just 131 kg. Apart from its lightweight construction, the motorcycle even carries a fairly powerful engine that is good for 18.90 BHP at 10,500 rpm and 14 Nm at 9000 rpm. It offers the lowest seat height of 785 mm among the others while even carries the first in class feature of keyless ignition. All the bikes here are using digital instrument consoles for the purpose.
